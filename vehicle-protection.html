Vehicle Protection

Preserve your vehicle’s showroom finish and protect your investment with expert care and premium protection solutions from Autolux.

  • Paint Protection Film (PPF)

    Preserve your vehicle’s flawless finish with professionally installed, high-performance paint protection film. Shield against rock chips, scratches, salt, and road debris. Ideal for daily drivers, performance vehicles, and long-term value retention.

  • Ceramic Coatings

    Elevate your car’s shine with advanced ceramic technology. This long-lasting, hydrophobic layer repels dirt and water, protects against UV rays and oxidation, and keeps your vehicle cleaner between washes. All with a showroom-level gloss.

  • Wheel, Glass & Trim Protection

    From brake dust buildup to weathered rubber and faded trim, we protect every surface of your vehicle. Our comprehensive application guards against the elements, ensuring lasting clarity, color, and finish on wheels, windows, plastics, and rubber.

  • Service Coordination

    Autolux handles all logistics, from booking the right provider to ensuring quality standards. We partner with only trusted, certified installers so you can expect consistent, flawless results without lifting a finger.

Maintenance & Service Tracking

Autolux ensures your vehicle stays in peak condition by managing every aspect of service scheduling and maintenance tracking.

  • Service History Management

    We keep a comprehensive, centralized record of all services performed on your vehicle from oil changes to tire replacements, making it effortless to maintain resale value and keep your car in peak condition. No more digging for receipts or forgotten service dates.

  • Maintenance Alerts

    Stay ahead of every required service with timely alerts for oil changes, tire rotations, inspections, and factory-recommended maintenance. Autolux tracks your intervals so you never miss a beat or risk unnecessary wear.

  • Pickup & Drop-off

    Autolux makes maintenance completely hands-off. We’ll pick up your vehicle from your home or office, handle the service coordination, and return it cleaned and ready so your day stays uninterrupted.

  • Preferred Vendor Network

    Your vehicle deserves the best, which is why we work exclusively with a vetted network of top-rated mechanics and certified service centers. Whether it’s a luxury sedan or exotic performance car, you can trust the care it receives.
